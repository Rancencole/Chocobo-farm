Thoughts on Web Accesibility

With the internet, there's a sense of unity. Currently, according to Monsido.com/web-accesibility, over 15% of the world's populations have disabilities. The most common disibilities are related to visual and hearing dissibilities. These limitations can lead to so many people being limited in their online experience as a user. Which is why it's so important to alot time to focus on them and their struggles.
